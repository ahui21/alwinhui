I see climate as the next big frontier that will drive innovation in a wide berth of industries from fintech to mobility to healthcare. I'm excited to help existing top-tier traditional VC firms harness their comprehensive resources in one of the fastest growing and most promising sectors in venture capital.

For the past couple years, I've been advising multiple venture capital funds on building out their climate portfolio from scratch. This included serving on the investment committees analyzing 250+ climate startups, advising seed and Series A portfolio companies, and building out the first-ever climate thesis and strategy for an international $300+ AUM fund through both an international and U.S.-focused lens.

Previously, I co-founded and built two startups in the biotech space, developing AI-based cloud platforms and medical devices for diagnosing and treating cancer, and previously co-led the Global Aviation Practice at Deloitte Consulting, managing the future strategy for 170+ airline clients across 30+ countries worldwide.

I graduated from Johns Hopkins University with a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering and a M.S. in Computer Science. I am currently pursuing my M.B.A. from the Stanford Graduate School of Business and an M.S. in Environment and Resources from the Stanford Graduate School of Sustainability.
